Supplies Needed

Students are expected to bring a pen or pencil, A 3-prong folder (to be left in class), earbuds, a flash drive, and an ipad to class every single day!.

Contributions for the class are always needed and welcome! Supplies like flashdrives, headphones, old mice or keyboards, sanitizing wipes, and any extra supplies will be put to good use!
