7th Grade Math

Welcome to the go-to spot for all of Ms.Walker's 7th grade math resources and materials

All assignments and projects for this course will be avaiable through Google classroom. You can access the Google classroom by clicking on the link on the left side of the page. The Google Drive folders with the content from class is linked at the side of the page as well. Note that you can only access the folder if you are signed in to your school gmail account. The calendar above is linked to Google classroom as well. Students are required to keep track of and complete their daily assignments. The assignment for class each day are listed on the Google Classroom site. All activities are listed as "Assignments". We will typically begin the assignment in class and whatever students don't finish in class is considered homework. Assignments from the previous day will be checked for completion the next day. If I understand that a particular assignment is causing a lot of undue stress on students, I will modify the assignment. Please feel free to let me know if your student is struggling. I understand that math can be a stressful subject, and though I think it is important to try hard and cover all material necessary, I am willing to make conessions when the consensus is that an assignment is too much to complete.

Supplies Needed

Students Students are expected to bring a pencil, binder with dividers, tabs, scissors, glue colored pencil, earbuds, ipad, and calculator.

For the Classroom Contributions for the class are always needed and welcome! Supplies like kleenex, looseleaf paper, glue, scissors, colored pencils, sanitizing wipes, and any extra supplies will be put to good use!
