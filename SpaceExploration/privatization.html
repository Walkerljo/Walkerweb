Orbital ATK

Creates and designs flight systems and defense systems along with space systems.
An industry-leading provider of commercial satellites used for global communications and high-resolution Earth photography.
Also a leading provider of small-medium sized aircrafts that perform scientific research, weather monitoring, and national security missions for government customers.
A pioneer in space logistics including shipments to the International Space Station for NASA and commercial orbital satellite servicing.
Also a developer of advanced commercial space-launch systems.
Premier provider of different spacecraft components, subsystems, and specialized engineering services.
