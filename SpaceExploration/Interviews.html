Loren Shriver is an astronaut who has been on three missions for NASA. He was an Air Force Colonel before being selected to become an astronaut. He was the pilot for the STS-51-C in 1985. In 1990, he commanded the crew that deployed the Hubble Telescope. His last space mission was in 1992 when he commanded the STE-46. He has won many medals and is in the astronaut hall of fame.
