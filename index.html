Hi, welcome! My name is Lindsay Walker.

Educator, Nerd, Technology Enthusiast
This is my journey

Raised in Madison, WI

Raised in a beautiful college town, I attended school in Madison from Elementary until high school graduation.  
 
 

Undergraduate Degree at the University of Wisconsin

I attended school in Milwaukee, WI and obtained a B.S. in education with minors in math and literature. After graduation, I taught math in Milwaukee's urban schools for 2 years.

Adventures Abroad

I took a position in a small International school in Venezuela for two years, teaching 6th grade math and science, as well as an 11th grade college prep math course.  
 

Back to Wisconsin

After conditions became very unstable in Venezuela, I returned to Madison and taught bilingual Algebra at West H.S. while preparing for my next step.
 
 

M.Ed. in STEM Education from UT-Austin

Studying hard for a year and a half, I taught math full time at Murchison M.S. while learning about assessment and Computational Thinking at UT-Austin.
 

Found a New Home In Austin

I have come to love everything the austin area has to offer. My Graduate studies were only the first step on this journey to discover and innovate in the field of technology education
 

A little bit about Lindsay

I live to learn and be challenged. I find a thrill and purpose in life constantly overcoming challenges I set for myself, and growing by gaining new knowledge.

The product of many of my own recent challenges can be found in the portfolios on my website, and in the living, breathing classrooms that this website is a portal for.
